# Naming Research

## Final Decision

Use `TwentyGuard` as the public brand and keep `20-20-20` in the subtitle,
README keywords, website title, and App Store metadata.

Recommended public line:

```text
TwentyGuard - Strict 20-20-20 breaks for macOS.
```

## Why Not Keep `20-20-20` As The Main Name

`20-20-20` is a strong search keyword, but weak as a brand. Search results are
crowded with rule explanations, medical pages, older open-source projects, and
many similarly named apps. It also makes word-of-mouth awkward: "I use
20-20-20" sounds like a rule, not a product.

## Why `TwentyGuard`

- Keeps the `Twenty` association with the 20-20-20 rule.
- Adds the product's real differentiation: guardrails and enforcement.
- Works for both break enforcement and night screen lock.
- Short enough for a menu bar app, GitHub repo, landing page, and App Store name.
- More distinctive than generic names like `Eye Break`, `Eye Timer`, or
  `Screen Rest`.

## Names To Avoid

- `LookAway` - already occupied by a strong Mac break-reminder product.
- `EyeGuard` - already used by eye-health and 20-20-20-adjacent products.
- `GazeGuard` - occupied by privacy/screen-blur products and gaze-related brands.
- `ScreenGuard` - occupied by screen-time and screen-protection products.
- `PaceGuard` - occupied in medical/device and software-monitoring contexts.
- `RestCue` - collision and pronunciation risk.
- `BreakGuard` - generic and already has scattered usage.

## Candidate Notes

| Name | Direction | Current Judgment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `TwentyGuard` | Strict 20-20-20 guardrails | Best current choice |
| `TwentyRest` | Softer eye-rest brand | Usable, but less differentiated |
| `ScreenPace` | Screen rhythm and pacing | Clean, but less tied to 20-20-20 |
| `GazePace` | Eye-focused rhythm | Risk of implying gaze tracking |
| `RestGuard` | Rest enforcement | Too generic |

## Remaining Checks Before Broad Launch

- App Store Connect app name availability.
- Domain registration availability.
- Basic trademark search in key launch markets.
- Search-result review for the exact name and similar spellings.

Record dated results in `evidence/naming-checks.md`. Do not treat this document
as a live legal or availability check; it captures positioning judgment, not
current clearance.
